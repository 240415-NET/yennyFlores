A library checkout system for users and book tracking.
-User can create a account with library via console
-User can interact to search a book with console
-User can interact to see the top best books for the month
-User can checkout books they like
-User can view all book that are checkout at moment, and list is by cloest checkout date. Due soon status.
-User can check in books, with status of past due or on time.
